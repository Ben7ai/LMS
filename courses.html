IT6223
Data Communications and Networking 2

This computer science course focuses on organizing and manipulating data efficiently, designing algorithms, and understanding essential data structures. Students develop problem-solving skills, algorithmic thinking, and optimize code for better performance, benefiting software developers.
ITE6201
Data Structure and Algorithms

The Data Structures and Algorithms course equips students with the fundamental tools to efficiently organize and manipulate data, as well as design and analyze algorithms for solving computational problems. Through this course, students learn how to optimize resource usage and develop problem-solving skills crucial for software development and computer science applications.
IT6205
Information Assurance and Security 1

Information Assurance and Security 1 is a course that introduces students to the principles and practices of securing digital information and systems. Participants gain an understanding of various threats, vulnerabilities, and strategies to safeguard data, networks, and technologies, preparing them to mitigate risks and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive information.
IT6200
Introduction to Human Computer Interaction

"Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction" is a course that delves into the study of how people interact with technology, focusing on the design and evaluation of user interfaces for optimal user experience. Students explore principles from psychology, design, and computer science to create intuitive and effective interfaces, gaining insights into user-centered design processes and usability evaluation techniques.
CYBS6269
Software and System Security

"Software and System Security" is a course that delves into advanced techniques for safeguarding software applications and computer systems from various security threats. Participants learn about vulnerability analysis, intrusion detection, encryption, and security protocols, gaining expertise in identifying and mitigating vulnerabilities to ensure the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of software and systems.
